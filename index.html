Concert Uniforms

Chaparral

Concert uniforms will be provided by the school, dresses or tuxedos to be chosen by each individual student. Tuxedos include a bow tie and cummerbund. Students wearing dresses will need to provide their own flat or low heel, black dress shoes and black nylons/tights. No bra straps should be visible at any time. Students wearing tuxedos will need to provide their own white long-sleeved tuxedo shirt, black dress shoes, and black socks.

***Please see examples of acceptable dress shoes and tuxedo shirt below.

We will try to assign garments that are already hemmed to the correct length for each student, however, if a shorter length is required students/parents are responsible for arranging for a professional hemming of the pants or dresses.

***All uniforms are expected to be returned at the end of every school year freshly dry-cleaned with a receipt dated after the last performance.

Cocopah

Concert Dress is formal and uniform for all concerts: Black dress shirt with sleeves, black skirt past the knees or black dress pants, black closed-toe dress shoes, black socks. Please make sure you follow the dress code for concerts. Please order early to allow time for shipping.

***Please see examples of acceptable dress attire below.

School Music Department shirts are expected to be worn for all field trips and on Spirit Days. Students going on the Disneyland trip need TWO t-shirts, one for each day in Disneyland.

Ordering information will be distributed through ParentSquare the first few weeks of school.
